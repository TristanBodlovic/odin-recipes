Ceci est la recette d'un bon boeuf bourgignon

Boeuf Bourguignon is a traditional French stew made with beef braised slowly in red wine, along with onions, carrots, garlic, and mushrooms. It’s rich, flavorful, and tender, often served with potatoes or crusty bread. This dish is a classic of French cuisine, known for its deep, comforting taste.

Ingredients

  • Beef for stewing
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 200 ml of red wine

Tasks

  1. Cut the beef into cubes, season with salt and pepper.
  2. Brown the beef in a pot with a little oil.
  3. Add the onion, carrots, and garlic, and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Sprinkle with flour and mix well.
  5. Pour in the wine and broth, then add the mushrooms.
  6. Cover and simmer on low heat for 1.5–2 hours until the meat is tender.
  7. Serve hot with potatoes or bread.
